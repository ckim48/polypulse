About PolyPulse

At PolyPulse, we believe in building stronger emotional connections through better communication. Our platform analyzes both text and images for emotionally harmful language and suggests kinder, more empathetic alternatives.

Designed for families, educators, and therapists, our AI fosters emotional intelligence in daily life.
